Activities

The Coding Club prides itself on its activities and programs. Our club focuses on being an inclusive place for programmers of all levels - as such, activities vary based on skill level and fit to the individual. The club hosts several teaching sessions throughout the year both in-school and out of school. Additionally, the club is a place where programmers can come together to work on projects and create an end-product. Finally, the club coordinates with other clubs with programming elements such as the Game Dev Club.

About us

The Maria Carillo Coding Club is run by a small team of dedicated programmers who are experienced in several coding languages and styles. We aim to teach students about the technologies which shape our modern world. The club teaches various programming styles including web design, game development, and app-design.
